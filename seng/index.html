Mission and Scope

Brief overview of the Tenda Project
What problem does Tenda address?
  • Public Procurement in Kenya is opaque and untrackable.
  • The Tenda project proposes to make all public procurement (initially only the portions reserved for Women, Youth and Persons with Disability) public available on the web and via mobile app.
What is the goal of Tenda?
At the end of this project:
  • We will have a dynamic website pupolated in realtime
  • A Mobile app for consumers of the data
  • A desktop app that can be used to populate data
What is the scope of this project?
Principal Deliverables are going to be:
  • Product management (Design and User Experience Optimization)
  • Software Engineering and Software Development
  • Marketing and adoption support
  • Technical support of MDAs and Consumers
